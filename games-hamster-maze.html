hamster maze


this game was created by a group of 3 students to complete a web game development course at our university. 'hamster maze' is a game for two players with a static camera showing from above the entire field of play, which is the maze.

each player can choose their hamster character, with which they must collect 7 fruit objects faster than the opponent. players must watch out for candy-type obstacles falling from the sky. the goal of selecting the prizes and obstacles in this way was to subtly promote healthy eating among gamers, with children as the main target audience.

currently the game has two levels designed with different mazes, however the project can be easily expanded with more levels. when the game starts, the levels are randomly selected for players.

some of the assets in the game, such as music and sounds, were downloaded from places offering free game resources. I was mainly responsible for designing graphics and modeling all 3D objects in blender as well as programming selected scripts.
