your wardrobe


the cross platform application that helps us organize our wardrobe. this application allows us to save (using sqlite) photos of clothes under the appropriate categories. each piece of clothing can also be assigned in the calendar on the day we want to wear it. additional functionalities of the application are also marking your favorite clothes and drawing random pieces of clothing as an inspiration for new outfits.
