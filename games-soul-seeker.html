soul seeker


this project was created as a part of practicing the "what would Walt do" method in level design. the level was designed in a style inspired by Studio Ghibli films, and the 3D models were downloaded from websites offering free game assets. through this project, I had the opportunity to practice post-processing in the Unity Standard Pipeline. additionally, I experimented with using occlusion culling.

the goal of the game is to explore an abandoned Japanese island and search for lost spirits, which must be guided through the torii gate.
