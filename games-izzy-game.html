izzy game


the game was created for my friend Izzy's 23rd birthday. the game includes all my friend's favorite things. that's why when playing as a duckling we collect 23 piggy banks that give us points and we avoid cigarettes that take our lives. to gain extra lives, we collect noodles, and after finding a sock, we can move around on the grass. our results are saved in the ranking, in which the number of collected piggy banks counts first, and then the time in which we managed to pass the entire board.

in this project I wanted to try to learn how to create a different kind of controller for the player and camera than I have done so far, and also to try to create a shader for water.

the game was created in the unity engine, most of the assets in the game were taken from free packages in the unity store. since my friend is a fan of Tom Odell, the music for the game consists of a playlist of her favorite top 10 songs by this artist. the sound effects were recorded with the help of a friend using audacity.
